Grilled Chicken Avocado Salad

by David Zinczenko and Matt Goulding

Description

You won't ever have to worry about not feeling full after eating this superfood-packed salad.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz cooked chicken
  • 12 cups arugula (1 prewashed bag)
  • 1/4 cup dried cranberries
  • 1 avocado, pitted, peeled, and sliced
  • 1/4 cup crumbled goat cheese
  • 1/4 cup walnuts, roughly chopped
  • 1/4 cup honey mustard vinaigrette
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Combine the chicken, arugula, cranberries, avocado, goat cheese, walnuts, vinaigrette, salt, and pepper in a large bowl, using your hands or 2 forks to fully incorporate the dressing.

Extra: how to pit an avocado:

  1. Work the blade carefully around the pit.
  2. Thwack the pit with blade; twist and carefully remove.
  3. Slice or dice, then spoon out the soft flesh.
